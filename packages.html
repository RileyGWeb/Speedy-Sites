What's so special about my websites anyway?

“You’re not just hiring me to build your website. You’re hiring me to maintain it, forever.”

Your website package doesn’t just come with a website: it comes with me. I stick around to be your website guy, forever. Running your business puts a thousand things on your plate to worry about every day; your website should be the last thing on your mind. Let a professional handle that for you, way below agency rates.


Browsers, operating systems and ISPs are constatnly updating themselves. Just because no changes were made to your website doesn’t mean it’s not suddenly broken due something outside of your control. Because of this law of the web universe, I perform wellness checks on my websites every two weeks. I send you a report on what (if anything) I find, and how your website will continue to be protected in the future.


I specialize in a very specific type of website: static, small business websites. I do this for a very important reason: I am just a guy. Typical web design agencies won’t deal with small business websites because they don’t bring in enough revenue. They want to sell big-ticket sites to large businesses for huge wads of cash. I know that because I work at one! During the day, I work on websites for huge organizations. Our cheapest rate is $150/hr. Yes, per hour. This is typical for a design agency, and is almost always out of the realm of possibility for a small business.


That’s where I come in. I don’t have to support the huge costs of running a design agency. I already have a day job, this is just my passion and creative outlet. I am here to make you a stunning, lightning-fast website for a price that actually fits your budget. Not only that, I’m going to stick around for you to ensure your web precense is always established in a way that converts users to customers.
